## Day 6 With a Challenge

Hello, fellow coders and readers of "My Progress Log"! Today, I'm excited to share my experience of completing day 6 of the free Hacking With Swift - SwiftUI course.

![](https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1705519134035/286b0fe3-4ed4-4941-9c55-f3ecbfc76fbf.jpeg align="center")

As with every day in this journey, the course, expertly guided by Paul Hudson, continued to offer a wealth of interesting and practical information. Paul has a knack for making complex concepts accessible, and his engaging teaching style keeps the learning process enjoyable and motivating.

The highlight of today's session, though, was tackling the classic “FizzBuzz” challenge. For those unfamiliar, FizzBuzz is a popular programming task, often used in job interviews, that tests a developer's ability to think logically and write clean, efficient code. The challenge involves printing a sequence of numbers, but for multiples of three, you print “Fizz” instead of the number, for multiples of five, you print “Buzz”, and for numbers which are multiples of both three and five, you print “FizzBuzz”.

Taking on FizzBuzz was not just an exercise in coding, but also a fun and engaging puzzle-solving experience. It was fascinating to see how such a seemingly simple task could be approached in various ways, each reflecting a different thought process and problem-solving strategy.

SwiftUI's streamlined and intuitive nature played a significant role in how I approached the challenge. It allowed me to focus more on the logic rather than getting bogged down with the syntax or other intricacies of the language.

The satisfaction of getting my code to successfully output the correct sequence was immense. It's moments like these that remind me why I love coding: the joy of solving problems, the thrill of writing code that works, and the constant learning and improvement.

As I continue with the Hacking With Swift - SwiftUI course, I look forward to more such challenges and learning experiences. Stay tuned for more updates as I delve deeper into the world of SwiftUI!

Until next time, keep coding and keep exploring!
